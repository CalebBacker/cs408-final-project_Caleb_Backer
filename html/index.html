Brick Breaker

Welcome!

Brick Breaker is a simple web game where you bounce a ball off your paddle to destroy all the bricks. Clear all the bricks to advance to the next level, rack up points, and compete on the global leaderboard (coming soon via AWS).

Main Menu

How to Play

  • Use A / D or Left / Right Arrow keys to move the paddle.
  • Keep the ball in play and break all the bricks to advance levels.
  • You have a limited number of lives. If you lose them all, it's game over.
